Conversation Norms I brainstormed...

  • Share only what you feel comfortable sharing.
  • When you share, share from the heart & share your own experience.
  • Tell ‚ÄúI‚Äù stories, not ‚Äúyou‚Äù stories or stories about other people outside the group.
